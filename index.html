About

Why BuildWithAwan exists

I'm Awan — a Computer Science student who got tired of just watching tutorials and reading documentation. At some point, I realized that consuming content without building anything was a trap. You feel productive, but nothing ships.

So I started building. Publicly. With real projects, real problems, and real deadlines. BuildWithAwan is my way of holding myself accountable — and hopefully, inspiring others to do the same.

My interests span from AI engineering and mobile development to reverse engineering and automation. I don't believe in specializing too early. I believe in building things that teach you what you need to learn next.

The long-term vision is clear: build software products that people actually use, and eventually establish a software & AI company. Every line of code I write today is a step toward that.

Flymate

A Flutter-based application combining BLE connectivity with GPS tracking for real-time device monitoring and location services.

Problem

Reliable BLE device tracking with accurate GPS positioning in a single cross-platform app didn't exist for the target use case.

Solution
